How many people die of Cholera each year?

Answer: 100,000-140,000

Where does Cholera affect the most people?

Answer: Crowded areas with poor sanitation, such as remote villages, refugee camps, and conflict zones.

Cholera can be prevented with vaccines. However, areas affected by cholera are often remote or poor, and lack resources. You can ask your parents to donate to doctors without borders, an organizatio that gives vaccines to people in areas affected by cholera. Click Here to donate
