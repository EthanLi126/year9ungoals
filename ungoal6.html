UN Goal 6 is "Clean Water and Sanitation". Many people around the still do not have clean water or places where they can wash their hands, take a shower, and use a washroom.

Clean water and being clean is something we all take for granted. When you go home, you can drink a glass of water, take a shower, and go to the toilet. However, In 2015, 29 percent of the global population didn't have clean and safe drinking water supplies. Thats over 2 billion people! On top of that 892 million people could not use, or did not have toilets.
